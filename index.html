Welcome to Persian Proverbs

Welcome! Here, you'll find thousands of timeless proverbs that beautifully reflect the deep wisdom and rich cultural heritage of our country. Each saying carries a story, a lesson, or a glimpse into everyday life and values. For our non-Persian-speaking visitors, we’ve included clear English translations along with thoughtful explanations to help you fully understand and appreciate their meanings. Take your time, scroll through, and immerse yourself in the world of Persian wisdom and tradition. Enjoy the journey!

Featured proverbs

زه گهواره تا گور دانش بجوی
Seek knowledge from the cradle to the grave.
گر صبر کنی ز غوره حلوا سازی
If you are patient, you can make halva out of sour grapes.
